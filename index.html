Summary

I work on data-driven modeling and analytics across interconnected systems, including resilience and reliability questions for critical infrastructure. For the authoritative and complete publication list, see my Google Scholar profile.

Research interests

  • Large-scale graph mining and analytics
  • Big data systems and architectures
  • Information retrieval and recommender systems
  • Critical infrastructure resilience and interdependency
  • Applied machine learning for scientific and operational data

Selected projects
